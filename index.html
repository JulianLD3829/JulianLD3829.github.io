Roadmap for 6.0

Days before zero, you may have access to the Canary Channel.

Day Zero will be slightly buggy, and some major features will not be released yet. Menus will be bland.

DeskUtility 6 will be second to use, but will be availible on the first screen of DeskUtility 5 as "Try New Version"

Day 7 will enable the Canary channel, and major features will be availible there. DeskUtility will be less buggy.

DeskUtility 5 is moved to the second bin.

Day 14 will enable some major features in the Experimental Options menu, and others will be fully released.

DeskUtility will be close to bug-free. Menus are less bland.

Day 21 will enable all major features, and all bugs will be ironed out. DeskUtility 7 development starts.

DeskUtility 6 updates take second priority, and less updates for 6 will be released.

Day 28 will be the final major updates for DeskUtility 6.0, and heavy development on 7 will have started three days before.

Day 35, Canary version of 7.0 is released and DeskUtility 6 will not get updates.
